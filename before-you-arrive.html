Before you arrive

A short checklist for planning a move to the UK.

Live

Live overviewBefore you arriveAfter you arriveLiving in LondonDisclaimer

Get your documents ready

Keep key documents in digital and paper form. This may include your passport, qualifications, references and right-to-work records.

Plan your budget

The UK can be expensive. London is usually the most expensive city. Plan for travel, deposit costs, daily travel and your first months of living expenses.

Research where you will live

Think about cost, commute and safety. A lower rent can still be expensive if travel costs are high.

Know your first steps

  • Find temporary or long-term housing
  • Check local transport
  • Understand healthcare registration
  • Prepare for job searching or study
