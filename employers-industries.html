Major employers and industries

Not all employers hire in the same way. This page helps explain the main employer types and the industries that shape the London market most strongly.

Understanding major UK and London industries

London has major clusters in finance, business services, technology, creative industries, health, higher education, tourism and public administration. Other UK cities may be stronger in manufacturing, logistics, regional public services or specialist industry bases.

Employer types

Public sector: government, NHS, local authorities, schools and universities. These employers often use formal person specifications and structured processes.

Private sector: companies of all sizes, from start-ups to global firms. These employers often move faster but can be less transparent.

Charity sector: mission-led organisations, foundations and service providers. These roles may value lived understanding, communication and evidence of alignment with purpose.

What different employers often look for

Public and regulated employers often want evidence, standards and fit against specific criteria. Private firms may focus more on speed, outcomes, commercial value and clear role relevance. Charities often look for values, communication and practical delivery.

Understanding the culture of the employer can change how you present exactly the same experience.
