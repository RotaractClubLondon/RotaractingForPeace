Why this matters

International talent can help fill skills gaps, strengthen teams and add wider experience. Recruitment works better when employers look beyond familiar job titles.

Keep recruitment fair

  • Use plain job descriptions
  • Focus on skills and outcomes
  • Be careful with “UK-only” assumptions
  • Explain role expectations clearly

Support after hiring

Retention improves when employers give clear onboarding, explain workplace norms and support the practical first months.
