About this prototype

This Knowledge Hub is a replicable, expandable and mouldable framework that can adapt to the needs of specific national and regional contexts. More than a repository of information, it also includes a custom tool that can be customised to be relevant for other contexts. The framework is based on a theory of change that aims to increase the understanding of employment systems, address structural and cultural violence aimed at international migrants, and aim to provide a lens to achieve a future where we can reduce exclusion, precarity and mental health harm.
