Learn overview

This section is for people who want to study, upskill, improve English, or understand how learning can support a route into work. Some people arrive with strong qualifications but still need help translating them. Others need a shorter step first, such as professional English or UK-specific training.

The aim here is to make those options easier to understand without turning the page into a long technical guide.

Study and upskilling options

There is no single correct route. One person may need a university degree, another may only need a short course, and another may need sector-specific language and confidence. The best option depends on the gap you are trying to close.

Learning-to-work pathways
