Why London is different

London offers a large job market, strong transport links and wide international communities. It also has higher costs and more competition.

What to expect

Costs
Rent and travel can be high.
Opportunity
More sectors and employers.
Commutes
Travel time matters when choosing where to live.
Diversity
Many cultures, communities and languages.

Think beyond central London

You do not need to live in the centre. Many people balance rent, travel and job access by living further out.
