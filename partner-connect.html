Expression of interest

For a prototype, this can be a simple route for employers who want to hear more, test the framework or explore whether the tool could support a specific workforce context.

Collaboration or feedback

The framework is designed to be expandable and adaptable. That means collaboration can include sector feedback, local partnership ideas, employer testing, or suggestions for what information and language would help users most.

Suggested contact points

In the prototype stage, this can stay simple: a general contact route, a partner enquiry route and a short note inviting employers to explain what gap they are trying to solve.
