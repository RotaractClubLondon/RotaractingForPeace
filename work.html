Work overview

For many international applicants, the hardest part is not lack of talent. It is trying to understand how the UK labour market reads that talent, which employers can sponsor, which roles are regulated, and what kind of evidence employers trust.

This section turns one large topic into smaller pages. Each page focuses on a practical part of working life, so people can move from confusion to a clearer next step.
