How UK hiring often works

Many employers use online applications, shortlisting and formal interviews. Clear CVs, tailored applications and evidence of skills matter.

Common barriers

  • Unfamiliar job titles
  • Questions about overseas experience
  • Need for sponsorship
  • Need for registration in regulated roles

What employers look for

They often want clear examples of what you did, what results you achieved and how your experience fits the role.

London context

London has more roles, but also more competition. It can be a strong target if your sector has deep demand there.
